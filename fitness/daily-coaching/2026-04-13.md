## Tomorrow: Mesocycle 1 begins — Session 1 (Squat pattern)

Mesocycle 1 kicks off on April 14. Here's exactly what's on the schedule:

**Week 1 target:** 2 sets per block, RPE 6–7. This is a calibration week — your job is to find the right starting loads, not to push.

---

### Block A — Superset × 2 rounds
**A1: Goblet Squat** — 8–10 reps
Hold one DB at chest, feet shoulder-width. Pause 1s at the bottom to open hips. Heels elevated on plates/books if needed.

*20s transition*

**A2: Dead Bug** — 6 reps/side
Lower back pressed to floor. Extend opposite arm + leg slowly. Exhale as you extend. This is core control, not a speed drill.

*45s rest → back to A1*

---

### Block B — Plyometrics (standalone) × 2 rounds
**B: Jump Squat** — 6–8 reps
Squat to parallel, explode up, land soft through the full foot. Full reset between reps — these are not bounces. If legs are still heavy from the race, reduce to 5 reps and focus on landing quality.

*75s rest*

---

### Block C — Superset × 2 rounds
**C1: DB Floor Press** — 10–12 reps
Neutral or semi-neutral grip. Elbows ~45° from torso. Full ROM limited by floor — that's fine, it protects your shoulders.

*20s transition*

**C2: DB Bent-over Row** — 10–12 reps/arm
Hinge to ~45°, row to hip. Squeeze at top. Keep back flat.

*60s rest → back to C1*

---

### Block D — Finisher
**Band Hip Circle** — 12 reps each direction
Band around ankles, slight squat, circles. Hip stabilizer activation. Flow straight through with no rest.

---

**Total time:** ~17 min at 2 sets. That's the target for week 1.

---

## Load selection for Session 1

No previous data to go on — start conservative and treat this session as your baseline.

**Goblet squat:** Try 12–14kg to start. If that's RPE 5 or under after set 1, go up.
**Floor press:** Try 10–12kg/hand. Full ROM first — load second.
**DB Row:** Try 12–14kg/hand. Squeeze matters more than weight here.

Log everything with RPE and RIR. That data is the foundation for the entire mesocycle.

## Race week wrap-up

Your race week taper was well-structured: reduced volume, maintained intensity, full rest on Friday. That's the protocol. Whatever happened on the course today — good or bad — the training block ahead is where the real work starts.

Mesocycle 1 is 4 weeks of accumulation. Week 1 is deliberate, week 2–3 is where load increases, week 4 is deload. Stay patient with week 1.
